1.Pile Locator
	This tool helps you locate and inform BBMP about any garbage pile-up.
	Walk to the location of the pile-up(switch on Internet,switch off GPS).
	Your location is now displayed,which can be sent as an SMS by the click of a button.

2.Educator
	This section tells you all you want to know about the different kinds of waste and how to dispose them.
	Awareness is the first step towards change.

3.Video Library
	This section links you to videos of fellow citizens of Bangalore  and their experiences with waste management.

4.Contact
	This section consists of all the information you need of the required authorities-BBMP officials and NGO's.

5.How to use
	This section contains details about how to use the app.
